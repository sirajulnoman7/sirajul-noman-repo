Short Essay on Travelling As A Hobby 300 Words for Kids and Students in English

“Travel in the youngest sort,” Bacon, “Is a part of education; in the elder, a part of experience.” Some may think otherwise. To them, visiting churches and monasteries, castles and fortifications, antiques and ruins as well as libraries and colleges is a sheer waste of time. They may further say that one could read the accounts of these or watch the movies that cover the important places of the world. They forget that the touch of living and breathing reality gives a different type of sensation and satisfaction.
